It’s as simple as pasting a URL!

# Step 1:
Open Google Chrome

# Step 2
Copy and paste the URL below to open the notepad:
`data:text/html, <html contenteditable>`

# Step 3
The notepad will reset when you refresh the page! I’d recommend bookmarking it!
