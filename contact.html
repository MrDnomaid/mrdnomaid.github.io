Contact

Hello!

This is the page that tells you how to contact me, but you probably already knew that.

If you're a fan and want to chat, please hit me up on Twitter instead of emailing.

Before I give you my details, here's a list of things I will ignore and probably report as spam:

  • Brand deals. Sorry, I don't do paid promotion. Sending me something for free in response for an honest opinion is fine, however.
  • YouTube MCN invites. I don't care if you're "different" or "special," just fuc go away.
  • Web design/development and related. I don't need a brand new website, nor do I need an app. If I wanted one of either, I'd prefer to do it myself.
  • Questions. Just Google it.
  • Pyramid-schemes and other BS. I'm not stupid.
  • Generally, if you're sending it to hundreds of people, don't bother.

On the flip side, here's some things I will probably respond to:

  • Media. If you're some large-ish media company and want me to do something, that's OK.
  • Questions related to my stuff. I'd prefer you ask on Twitter, but emailing me questions related to myself is acceptable.
  • Free stuff. No matter what it is, I always like free stuff. If you want me to review it, I might, but note that I may not get around to it.
  • Anything else. Pretty much anything else that's not on the exclude list is fine, just don't send the email multiple times as that will just make Outlook report you as spam.

Great, that's it. My email is just tom [at] dnomaid [dot] co [dot] uk. Do mention that you read this list!

If it's something super confidential, send me an email and I'll give you my Telegram details. Note that I don't check Telegram much so my response may be delayed or non-existant.
