Name History

Pre-2012
Thomas The Train Conductor
Before I had internet access, I had (and still have) a Gateway PC running Windows 98. My username on it was 'Thomas The Train Conductor' presumably because at the time I wanted to be a train conductor. This name didn't last long though, and was replaced when I created my Google account.

2013
tomcar670fast
In 2013, I decided I needed a YouTube channel, but to make one of those, I needed a Google account. Originally I wanted just tomcar, but that was taken. So was tomcar670. Being the creative person I am, I just shoved 'fast' onto the end.

2014
_Diamond_Pigman
In 2014, my friend Oliver introduced me to Minecraft. I don't exactly remember why I chose this name, but I did. This name stuck with me for many years , going through many changes; DiamondPigman, Diamond_Pigman, D1amondPigman etc.

2016
dnomaid
In late 2015, I realised how stupid and childish 'DiamondPigman' sounded. Unfortunately, I still wasn't very creative, so I just used diamond backwards. I think it's pretty neat and much better than my old usernames.
